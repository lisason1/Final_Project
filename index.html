FAQs

Have a question? Verus has answers.

What is Verus and how does it work?

Verus is a virtual reality platform that delivers real-time live music experiences from anywhere in the world. With Verus, you can immerse yourself in a real-time show, concert, or festival setting.

How do I access Verus virtual reality music experiences? Do I need any special equipment to use Verus?

Verus is compatible with other VR headests including MetaQuest, Vive, and Playstation VR. If you do not have access to a headset, Verus will ship the Verus Headset to you after logging into your accunt. The Verus interface will appear after wearing the headset and searching for Verus.

Is Verus really free?

Yes, Verus is free. Verus focuses on making live music events accessible to everyone by offering a convenient, safe, and social experience to anyone. If you would like to upgrade your Verus experience, purchase our premium membership.

How does this virtual reality music experience compare to a real music event? Can I interact with other users?

We partner with artists and organizers to place our advanced high-quality audio and multi-camera technology in their events to relay and capture the same live energy and excitement in real-time. Our immersive experiences will feel as if you have teleported to the event and are actually there. You can interact with other users who are also attending the event with Verus, but will be unable to interact with others that are attending this event in person.
